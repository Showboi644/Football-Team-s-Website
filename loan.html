Fatih College F.C Considers Loaning Out Yaw Mensah

The management of Fatih College F.C has made the difficult decision to consider loaning out Yaw Mensah due to his inconsistent performances for the club. Despite his undeniable talent, Mensah's recent form has not met the expectations set by the team.

While recognizing Mensah's potential, the club believes that a temporary move to another team may provide him with the opportunity to regain his confidence and rediscover his top form. This decision has not been taken lightly, as Mensah has been a valued member of the squad for several seasons.

However, the club remains committed to supporting Mensah in his development and believes that this temporary arrangement will ultimately benefit both the player and the team in the long run.

"We believe in Yaw's abilities, but sometimes a change of scenery can be beneficial for a player's growth. We will continue to monitor his progress closely and look forward to welcoming him back stronger than ever." - Club Manager
