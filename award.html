Providence from Fatih College F.C Wins FIFA Women's Award

We are thrilled to announce that Providence, a key player from Fatih College F.C Women's Team, has been honored with the prestigious FIFA Women's Award for the calendar year 2024. This recognition is a testament to Providence's exceptional talent, dedication, and contribution to the sport.

Throughout the year, Providence has showcased exemplary performance on the field, inspiring her teammates and fans alike with her skill and leadership. Her remarkable achievements have not only brought glory to Fatih College F.C but have also raised the bar for women's football globally.

As a university team, this accolade highlights Fatih College F.C's commitment to nurturing and empowering talented athletes, particularly women, in the pursuit of their dreams. Providence's success serves as an inspiration to aspiring young players within the club and beyond.

"Providence's journey is a testament to the power of determination and perseverance. We are immensely proud of her accomplishments and look forward to witnessing her continued success on and off the field." - Fatih College F.C Women's Team Coach
